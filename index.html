Vedant Zope

I am a final-year undergraduate in Metallurgy and Materials Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, with a deep passion for advanced computational work. My career goal is to leverage this expertise in interdisciplinary domains.

Currently, I'm working on my thesis on Johnson-Cook Damage Model Calibration, guided by Professor Sumantra Mandal.

My research experience includes a project at Stanford University with Professor Pascal Geldsetzer, developing machine learning models for estimating Maternal and Child Health indicators.

In summer 2023, I conducted research at Aalto University, Finland, focusing on Swift Voce Law and ductile fracture parameter calibration, working on the Puhti high-performance cluster under Professor Junhe Lian.

In 2022, I worked on UAV Path Planning at the University of Birmingham under Prof D.T. Pham, considering weather conditions.

I was also an active member at TeamKART (Formula-Student Team) IIT KGP, involved in designing and manufacturing formula student prototypes.

Beyond academics, I am an avid swimmer, a track and field athlete with numerous medals, and an active trader and investor in global markets.

Publications
Implementation of the Bees Algorithm for UAV Mission Planning
Anubhab Dasgupta*, Vedant Zope*, Asrul Harun Ismail*,   14th International Seminar on Industrial and Management (ISIEM) Proceedings, 2023
manuscript

This publication extends our previous work to incorporate obstacle avoidance, resulting in a comprehensive mission planning algorithm. The algorithm encompasses routing optimization and obstacle avoidance, providing a fully-fledged approach to UAV mission planning.
Utilizing the Bees Algorithm and Machine Learning to Optimize the UAVs Travel Time in consideration of Weather Conditions
Vedant Zope*, Asrul Harun Ismail*,   BAA Workshop, 2022
paper | code

We presented a strategy for UAV Routing Optimization in consideration of weather conditions, using the Combinatorial Bees Algorithm and integrating it with a tuned machine learning model trained to consider the effects of external weather parameters.
