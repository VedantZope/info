Vedant Zope

I am currently a graduate student pursuing a Master’s degree in Computational Science and Engineering, with a focus on quantitative finance, at the University of Pennsylvania. I completed my Bachelor's of Technology (Honors) in Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur in 2024.

My research experience includes a project at the Stanford School of Medicine with Prof. Pascal Geldsetzer, where I worked on developing a machine learning model for estimating maternal and child health indicators in low- and middle-income countries using geotagged satellite imagery data.

In the summer of 2023, I conducted research at Aalto University in Finland, focusing on development of an end-to-end inverse parameter calibration pipeline for the Swift-Voce hardening law and the eMBW ductile fracture model, working on the Puhti HPC at Prof. Junhe Lian's lab.

In 2022, I worked on weather-dependent UAV path planning and routing optimization using the Bees Algorithm in collaboration with Prof. D.T. Pham from the University of Birmingham.

I was also an active member of TeamKART (Formula Student Team) at IIT KGP, contributing to the design and manufacturing of Formula Student prototypes for national as well as international competitions.

Beyond academics, I am an active derivatives trader and investor in global markets, an avid swimmer, and a track and field athlete with numerous medals.

Implementation of the Bees Algorithm for UAV Mission Planning
Anubhab Dasgupta, Vedant Zope, Asrul Harun Ismail
14th International Seminar on Industrial and Management (ISIEM) Proceedings, 2023
Paper

This publication extends our previous work to incorporate obstacle avoidance, resulting in a comprehensive mission planning algorithm. The approach encompasses routing optimization and obstacle avoidance, offering a fully fledged solution for UAV mission planning.

Utilizing the Bees Algorithm and Machine Learning to Optimize UAV Travel Time Under Varying Weather Conditions [Paper Presentation]
Vedant Zope, Asrul Harun Ismail
BAA Workshop, 2022
Report | Code

We presented a strategy for UAV routing optimization under varying weather conditions, using the combinatorial Bees Algorithm and a tuned machine learning model trained to account for external weather parameters.
